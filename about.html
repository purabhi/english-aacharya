Our methods are completely different and we do not use conventional methods of teaching. We solely believe in Alvin Toffler's learn, unlearn and relearn theory i.e. 'Learn, Unlearn, Relearn'.




The Method

At English Aacharya, we believe that our unique teaching method is key to making language learning a true success. It is based on decades of experience in language training and is continually being developed to match the needs of a changing world. The principles of this method are quite simple and make it easy for learners of any level.

Quality Not Quantity

Each class consists of not more than 12 students. This makes it easy to be heard and form friendly groups to practice with techniques such as pair and group work. Small groups also breed teamwork so your classmates are as helpful in learning as the instructor.




Learning By Understanding

Even our motto – ‘Learn .Unlearn. Relearn’ is centered around the need to continuously challenge our learning, to question what you see/learn, and to not just accept things as they are. At English Aacharya, a learner is encouraged to speak in the language he or she intends to learn. No cramming or complex theories about the language. Class is not only about lectures and answering your questions. Students take up almost 70 percent, if not more ‘talk time’ in class.

Everybody is different and so is the way they learn…

Before you start our journey with us, we test you for the level of knowledge you have about the language. It helps us understand your learning requirement. You join a small group of learners whose level of English matches yours. This makes each learner feel ‘equal’ and therefore comfortable with all other learners.




Encourage not criticize.

Instructors help you build confidence in the language by encouraging you to speak. They do not criticize you for making mistakes. You are expected to ask questions and clear your doubts every step of the way. You are guided and encouraged to correct yourself.

All work and no play, makes Jack a dull boy

The most effective way to learn is while having fun. Learning then does not become a painful task, rather it becomes an activity you look forward to. Here, instructors are friendly guides who encourage you to have fun while you learn.
