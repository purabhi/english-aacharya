Divya Joshi

Divya is a training wizard. He holds a germane experience of 10 years in the field of Sales and Training. He is an experienced trainer of Communication and his forte is English Language training. He has motivated, educated, mentored more than ten thousand students to IITs and IIMs and has been doing so since last ten years. He has been a guest faculty to BITS Pilani, BHU, IIT Kanpur, IIM Rohtak and has trained multinational corporates such as Vodafone India, Axis Bank, V-Mart and many Government Institutions such as Department of Agriculture, Jal Nigam, etc. for soft-skills, sales pitch, business etiquettes, brand building and effective communication. Divya has worked with brands like TIME, IMS Learning resources, Educomp, Pearson as a Verbal Ability/resource trainer and has conducted effective classroom sessions on Verbal Ability, Vocabulary and Reasoning for both students as well as teachers. Divya has also been a consultant to some of the top schools in UP and he has been successfully conducting the following sessions: 1. Teacher’s training 2. Motivational training 3. ELT training 4. POCSO (knowledge training) 5. Recreational workshops for Playschool teachers
